Global Diversity CFP Day

Saturday 27th January 2018

Safe, likeminded people. Focus on what the attendee will get from today. Your voice is your unique

--------

Have you always wanted to become a conference speaker?

Let 2018 be the year that you make that dream a reality!

On Saturday 27th January 2018 there will be numerous workshops hosted around the globe to encourage and advise newbie speakers on putting together their very first talk proposal.

Each workshop will have experienced speakers on hand to mentor you with your proposal and provide speaking advice!

What do you need to do next? Sign up to a workshop at a location near you and, if you have one, bring a laptop along to submit that proposal.

These workshops will not have a specific technology, devops or process focus. The aim is to encourage people from all underrepresented groups to apply to speak regardless of the boundaries of technology or communities.
